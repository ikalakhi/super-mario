It's a me, Mario

Mario is a legendary video game character known for his adventurous spirit and iconic appearance. Created by Nintendo, he's a cheerful plumber famous for his red cap, blue overalls, and mustache. Mario's captivating quests often involve saving Princess Peach from the villainous Bowser in imaginative worlds. With his jumping skills and power-ups, he's starred in various game genres and remains a timeless symbol of gaming fun.
